Red Flag Criteria
Call the surgical team for any of these
Call now: Fever ≥ 101.5°F, sudden severe pain beyond baseline, calf swelling or pain (DVT risk), wound drainage that is cloudy or foul-smelling, loss of sensation or circulation in foot.
Call same day: ROM acutely worse (not just a tough day), swelling that balloons despite icing, extension gap suddenly increases, unexpected new sounds from the joint.
